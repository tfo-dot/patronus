# 🪄 Patronus

> **"Expecto Communicationem"**

Patronus is a secure, decentralized LAN-only communicator designed for environments where traditional connectivity fails. Whether you are in Azkaban, an air-gapped facility, or facing a total infrastructure collapse, Patronus ensures the conversation continues.

## The Problem
In an era of ubiquitous open and untrusted wireless networks, the risk of sniffing (eavesdropping) and traffic modification is extremely high.

Furthermore, in specific conditions—such as air-gapped networks in military or industrial environments, or during telecommunications infrastructure failures—there is a critical need for secure and direct information exchange. Most modern messaging apps fail in these scenarios because they rely on a handshake with a centralized cloud server that is physically unreachable.

## The Solution
**Patronus** doesn't care about the internet. It operates entirely within the local subnet. If you can ping it, you can chat on it.

* **Zero External Dependencies:** No STUN/TURN servers, no cloud databases.
* **Peer-to-Peer / Local Server:** Optimized for low-latency communication over LAN.
* **Privacy by Isolation:** Your data never leaves the building because it physically can't.
